Research & Innovation

From early discovery through IND—we support the full research continuum with validated methods and collaborative R&D.

Focus Areas

Our Research Programs

Three integrated pillars: Discovery, Translation, and Innovation Labs. Each builds on rigorous science and collaborative project management.

Discovery Research

High-throughput screening, CRISPR screens, target validation, and mechanism-of-action studies. We integrate genomics (RNA-seq, ChIP-seq), proteomics, and phenotypic assays to deliver prioritized target lists and dose-response data.

  • CRISPR knock-out / knock-in screens
  • Compound and antibody screening
  • Biomarker discovery & prioritization
  • Target validation & MoA studies

Translational Science

Bridge preclinical models and clinical trials. We develop and validate fit-for-purpose assays (ELISA, ddPCR, NGS panels), PD/PK biomarkers, and companion diagnostics. GLP-compliant workflows for IND-enabling studies.

  • Biomarker assay development & validation
  • PD/PK and pharmacodynamic studies
  • Companion diagnostic design
  • Proof-of-concept trial support
