Overview

From Discovery to IND

We provide integrated scientific support for drug development—from early discovery through IND-enabling studies. Our team assists with mechanism-of-action studies, target validation, biomarker assay development and validation, pharmacokinetic/pharmacodynamic analyses, and documentation to support regulatory submissions.

Services are designed for GLP compliance where needed, with validated assays and audit-ready study reports. We work with small molecules, biologics, and cell/gene therapies. Typical engagements include assay development, preclinical study design and execution, bioanalytical method validation, and IND section preparation.
