Regulatory & Quality

FDA pre-submission meetings, 510(k) and PMA support, IVD design controls, quality management systems, and regulatory strategy for US, EU, and global markets.

Overview

Navigate Regulatory Pathways Confidently

Our regulatory and quality team supports IVDs, devices, and biologics through design controls, validation, and submission preparation. We help with FDA pre-submission meetings, 510(k) and PMA pathways, EU IVDR/IVDD compliance, and quality management systems (ISO 13485, 21 CFR Part 820).

We provide regulatory strategy, gap assessments, design history files, validation protocols and reports, and audit preparation. Whether you're developing a companion diagnostic, point-of-care test, or software as a medical device, we tailor support to your product and target markets.
